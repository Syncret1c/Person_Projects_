⇝ — Hyuga Hifumi (悠夢, 'One Two Three Places in The Sun') is a youngling born into the Main House from the current head of the Hyuga Clan. Being brought up within such a prestigious clan, Hifumi was met with harsh and strict treatment and training from his father, in order to perfectly mold Hifumi into a capable clan-head.. Though fortunately for Hifumi, the love and affection his father lacked was something he gained in his mother. After his harsh training sessions with his father, Hifumi would spend the rest of the day with his mother where she showed him compassion and taught him about the other qualities of being a leader. Due to these factors, Hifumi has grown to be a calm, collected, and understanding individual. In terms of strength, Hifumi is capable of utilizing Taijutsu and Ninjutsu at an efficiency comparable to that of a Chuunin. Among the clan elders it is rumored that Hifumi’s Byakugan is among one of the strongest they’ve ever seen, far surpassing that of most adults of the main branch.
